Extensive research and development experience

我们有丰富的研发经验

Computational Ethology

计算行为学

We have extensive research and development experience in the area of pose estimation, computer vision and image processing.

Computational Ethology is the study of behavior using Computer Vision and various automated computational techniques. Animal Pose estimation refers to the way we can detect the pose of the animal and analyze it. Animal pose estimation plays an essential role in learning and understanding animal behavior. Observing and efficiently describing behavior is a core tenant of modern ethology, neuroscience, medicine, and technology with endless applications in the field of security, surveillance, intrusion detection, and other video analytics.

This technology can also be adapted for various use-cases such as human pose estimation, which can help detect different security risks:

  • Fighting people
  • Falling people
  • Thiefs
  • Sick people

Hyperchip Technology在姿态估计、计算机视觉和图像处理领域拥有丰富的研发经验。动物姿势估计是指Hyperchip Technology的技术可以检测动物的姿势、其特征，然后对其进行分析的方法。该技术还可以用于各种不同的用例，例如：

  • 人类姿势预估和检测不同的安全相关风险
  • 人们将要开始战斗或者因事故或心力衰竭等摔倒
  • 所处在在未经授权的危险区域等
